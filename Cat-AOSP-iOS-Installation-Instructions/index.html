Sideloading Tools

  • 3uTools
  • AltServer
  • Sideloadly (Recommended ‚Äì supports auto-refresh)

Installation Steps

  1. Install iTunes and iCloud (non-Store versions).
  2. Install one of the sideloading tools listed above.
  3. Connect your iOS device to your PC.
  4. Open the sideloading tool and select the Cat-AOSP IPA.
  5. Sign the app with your Apple ID.
  6. Install the IPA to your device.
  7. On your device, go to:
    Settings ‚Üí General ‚Üí VPN & Device Management
  8. Trust the developer profile.
  9. Open Cat-AOSP.

üçé macOS

Requirements

  • macOS 10.14 or lower (iTunes required)
  • iCloud (pre-installed)

Sideloading Tools

  • 3uTools
  • AltServer
  • Sideloadly (Recommended)

Installation Steps

  1. Install your chosen sideloading tool.
  2. Connect your iOS device.
  3. Select the Cat-AOSP IPA inside the tool.
  4. Sign the app with your Apple ID.
  5. Install the IPA.
  6. Trust the developer profile in System Settings.
  7. Open Cat-AOSP.
